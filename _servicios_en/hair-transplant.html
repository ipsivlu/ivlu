What is a hair transplant?

It is a medical procedure that relocates healthy hair follicles from a donor area to areas affected by hair loss. We use the FUE technique, which leaves no visible scars and provides permanent results.

The initial evaluation can be done virtually or in person and includes trichoscope analysis or guided photos. You choose where to begin!
