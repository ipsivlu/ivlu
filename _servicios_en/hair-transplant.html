Hair transplant: the definitive solution for hair loss

Hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves moving healthy hair follicles from a donor area to areas with hair loss. It's an effective option for restoring density in specific areas, offering natural and long-lasting results.

Benefits of hair transplant

01Restore volume in thinning areas: Ideal for zones with advanced hair loss, improving hair density.

02Natural results: By using the patient's own hair, a completely natural appearance is achieved.

03Quick recovery: With our technique, recovery is fast, with minimal discomfort and almost invisible scars.

Hair Transplant Phases

01Assessment and Design: The scalp is analyzed and a personalized plan is designed.

02Extraction and Implantation: Follicles are extracted one by one and implanted in the recipient areas.

03Results and Follow-up: At our Ivlu Medical Center, follow-up is conducted to ensure the success and healthy growth of the grafts.
