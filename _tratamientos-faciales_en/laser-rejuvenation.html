Your treatment journey: What to expect

Your comfort and safety are our top priorities. The procedure begins with the application of medical-grade numbing cream. During the next 30-45 minutes, our FDA-approved laser works with precision while you relax comfortably.

Post-treatment, you'll experience mild warmth and redness, similar to a light sunburn. Most patients return to their regular activities within 3-4 days, and we'll provide comprehensive aftercare instructions. You'll notice progressive improvement in your skin that continues over the following months as collagen production increases.
